Songs I Like

I never really thought about what my absolute favorite song is, because the more I try to answer that, the harder it becomes. Some songs are tied to memory; some are tied to mood; some you only understand years later. Music ends up becoming a quiet archive of who I was, who I am, and who Im trying to become. So rather than forcing a hierarchy, this page is just a slowly growing map of the tracks that stayed with me for reasons I dont always know how to explain.
